Chapter 47

Uncle Remus And Mr. Cable

MR. JOEL CHANDLER HARRIS ('Uncle Remus' was to arrive from Atlanta at seven o'clock Sunday morning; so we got up and received him. We were able to detect him among the crowd of arrivals at the hotel-counter by his correspondence with a description of him which had been furnished us from a trustworthy source.
